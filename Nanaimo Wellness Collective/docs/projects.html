Programs & Case Studies

Although Nanaimo Wellness Collective is a fictional studio, the following case studies represent realistic examples of how we could support different clients.

1. Exam Season Reset – Student Wellness Series

Client type: Local university student wellness office
Goal: Reduce exam-related stress, improve sleep, and offer healthy coping tools.

Program design:

  • 4-week series of 60-minute Gentle Flow classes
  • Short guided meditation recordings for home use
  • Printable “study break stretch” poster shared in residence halls

Outcomes:

  • 92% of students reported feeling less stressed after class
  • Many students requested that the series run during midterms as well
  • The wellness office added the program to its ongoing student support offerings

University students often experience academic stress — our programs support healthier coping.

2. Desk to Decompress – Corporate Wellness Workshop

Client type: Marketing agency with desk-based staff
Goal: Decrease discomfort from prolonged sitting and support employee well-being.

Program design:
- One 90-minute on-site workshop at the client’s office
- Education on posture, ergonomics, and breathwork
- A short desk-friendly movement sequence requiring no special equipment

Outcomes:
- Employees reported increased body awareness and simple tools they could apply during the workday
- HR received positive feedback and booked quarterly follow-up workshops

Many employees experience pain and tension from long hours at a desk — this workshop teaches simple tools to ease discomfort.

3. Nervous System Reset – Small Group Series

Client type: Small group of friends navigating burnout and life transitions
Goal: Create a regular, supportive space for gentle movement and nervous-system regulation.

Program design:
- 6 weeks of private small-group classes in the studio
- Combination of restorative yoga, supported stretching, and guided journalling prompts
- Optional at-home breathing practices between sessions

Outcomes:
- Clients reported feeling more grounded and connected
- Improved sleep and reduced stress across the group
- Several participants continued weekly sessions afterward

A supportive small-group environment helps clients reconnect, reset, and regulate their nervous systems.
